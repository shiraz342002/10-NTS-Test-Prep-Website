### Key Features <a name="key-features"></a>


- **Extensive Question Bank: Access a wide variety of multiple-choice questions from past NTS papers, covering subjects such as English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Computer Science.**
- **Interactive Quizzes: Engage with interactive quizzes that simulate the actual NTS test environment. Answer questions within a set time limit to improve your time management skills.**
- **Score Tracking: Keep track of your progress with our scoring system. After each quiz, see your score and review correct answers to understand where you need improvement.**
- **Timer for Each Question: Each question comes with a one-minute timer to help you practice answering questions under time constraints, similar to the actual exam conditions.**
- **Detailed Explanations: For every question, detailed explanations are provided to help you understand the concepts and reasoning behind the correct answers.**
- **Quiz Guidelines: Read through our quiz guidelines to understand the rules and structure of the quizzes. Ensure you are well-prepared and know what to expect during the actual test.**
- **User-Friendly Interface: Our website features a clean, intuitive design that makes it easy to navigate and find the resources you need.**
